Making Tea

1.Heat the water.

2.Put the tea in the teapot filter.

3.Wour the water into the kettle and set the timer

4.Remove the tea leaves and enjoy!

Making Paratha and Aloo curry

1.Take wheat flour in a bowl,add in salt.

2.Add warm water into the wheat flour and make into a soft dough.

3.Knead the dough for 5 to 8 minutes till it smooth and soft.

4.Cover the dough with a plate or plastic wrap and set aside for 30 minutes to 1 hour.

Making Biryani

1.Put the boiling water washed rice, boil for 5 minutes and drain.

2.Layer the chicken mixture the chicken mixture repeat this twice

3.Pour the water directly onto the tea bagarnish with cumin seeds, and ginger.

4.Lower the heat and simmer for a further 10 minutes.
